About Lilavatibai Podar High School



Lilavatibai Podar High School, is a co-educational English medium, unaided institution founded in 1987 by late Shri Ganesh Narayan Podar. The school was named after Late Shrimati Lilavatibai Podar, daughter-in-law of our founder and mother of our patron Late Shri Ganesh Podar.
The School is fully owned and managed by the Anandilal and Ganesh Podar Society. The Educational Complex was founded under the inspiration of Late Shri Pitashree Anandilal Podar in 1927 as a small exclusive school, for all those who love their children and want to impart quality education.
The School has classes from Primary Grade 1 upto ISC Grade 12. The school is registered with the Maharashtra State Education Department and is affiliated to the CISCE Council for the Indian Certificate of Secondary Examination, New Delhi. The school strives to impart sound, liberal education aimed at the development of the total personality of child.
The organizational structure, the board of governors and staff are appointed by the trustees. The functions and process are structured and distributed through a formally established subordinate network.
